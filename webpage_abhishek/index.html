Document Requirements for IEC

Pan Card

Valid PAN card of the business or the individual applying for IEC.

Identity Proof

Any one of Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport of the applicant.

Address Proof

Business address proof like electricity bill, rent agreement, or other valid documents.

Bank Proof

Cancelled cheque or bank certificate in the name of the applicant entity.
